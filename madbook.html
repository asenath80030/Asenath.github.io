Mad Book Gaming App

Casual competitive game designed for college students!

Concept

Mad Book is a casual competitive game designed for college students. Race against the clock and evil librarians to find books inside the library. Get rewards and add points to your university's score.

Brainstorm

I I want to create an environment that is familiar to students, this is one way to attract my target players. In this game, characters pick up books with different colors and categories. You sometimes need to tap the books on the screen occasionally, you must avoid all of these disasters events like earthquakes, fires, and thieves get in your way. what's more, There is a choice be single or multiplayer games, and you play games, offline or online and send the rewards to your friends instead of using them. If you pick the wrong book, you lose, if you take too long to pick a book, the librarian comes out and kicks you out and you lose.

Project Details
  • Type: Competitive Game
  • User: Casual Player
  • Story:Find the books inside library,familar themes to attract my target player.
