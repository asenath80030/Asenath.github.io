Man Force App

Online shopping app - Amazing deals for Man

INTRODUCTION

Amazing Deals For Men Force has one goal - to provide a simple, easy shopping experience for every man who wants to update the contents of his equipment. We do the heavy lifting by scouring the planet for the best toolbox. The equipment brands to bring you a shopping experience unlike any other. Now, we are taking that experience to another level of convenience with the Man Outfitters App.

DESIGN THINKING

This app was my first time designing an e-commerce experience that will offer great deals for men. and help them find their favorite products, Place orders, and easily track orders. Through the design process, after confirming core functions and features, I started sketches and wireframing to find out the right design idea before I jumped into hi-fi mockups. In the beginning, I mainly focused on designing 3 parts browsing, checkout and track orders.

Solution

Save money for Men by connecting directly with product manufacturers. Say goodbye to the middleman! Provides the best mobile shopping experience and offer the lowest prices in the industry.
