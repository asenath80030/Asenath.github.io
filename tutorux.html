Why I Create This App

Exchange Your Skill & Gain Free Tutor

I met a very old man during community activities. He was playing Jazz piano very beautiful. I asked him if He could teach me, how much lessons would cost, and where his studio is. He said he is a musician, he wants me to teach him how to cook and that way, he will teach me piano for free. By exchanging skills he became really good at cooking and I practiced more jazz piano. Sometimes we went to the same community event to give piano performances. We both all very happy! It's because of this experience that I decided to create Tutor Too App, This app that connects people with different skills so that they can exchange them for free.

The Core Value About This App

Exchange & Share

Today, interactions are usually often impersonal because of social media like facebook etc, and comment. To face to face, this app increases personal interactions and allows people to feel more supported within a community by exchanging skills. Everyone can have more chances to get tutoring or lessons so that they can polish skills and build real relationships in the real world.

Question & Solution

Let's Go Through Some Of The Keys

  • General Questions and Solutions

    • Finding a tutor to work with in person is often difficult. Often, it's hard to coordinate schedules, figure out a location that works for both people, and find a price that is manageable. People get tired of watching youtube or reading web pages to try to learn new things. Tutor Too will match people with others who have the skills they wish to swap. This app will allow users upon registration to list their skills they are willing to teach or to the skills they wish to learn. Users who do teach sessions will earn an hour of tutoring for every hour they spend teaching. They can use these hours to learn new skills from others and build up a real relationship with others in their same community. the Tutor Too app can help grow a strong community of curious hobbyists and learners.

  • What Do You Expect To Find In This App?

    • I would like to find people who are very talented and who have useful skills.

  • What do you care about?

    • I want it to be simple to set up an appointment. I don't want it to be hard to find a place, to fill out a form out, or to set a date. Solution: design a really good profile for users to learn details about their tutor's background.

  • What are most important things you want to see in the tutor's profile?

    • The Tutor's profile should demonstrate that they are trustworthy. Solution: Set up categories for entering work experience, education background a promotion board, the tutor can also upload their certifications and blog, connect to LinkedIn, show how many students they teach.

  • How do you think this app can make you want to exchange your skills?

    • I can gain free tutoring and meet different people in the same community. all my user think exchanging skills and gain free tutoring will work because there is a demand for it. Solution: design every page to be more related to sharing, so you can switch your status by moving the bar, find tutor or find student, set up the page for exchanging skills and show your free tutor hours.

  • Is distance important to you?

    • Yes, for free tutoring, you don't want to go too far. Solution This is why I design to search tutors by map and find tutor nearby you.
